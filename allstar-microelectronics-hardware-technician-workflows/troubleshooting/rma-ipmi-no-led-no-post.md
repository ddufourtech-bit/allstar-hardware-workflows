# RMA: IPMI No LED / No POST (H12DSi-N6)

**Status:** Resolved (RMA Issued)  
**Date:** March 2025

## Problem Summary
Board would not power on and showed no IPMI activity.

## Findings
- No IPMI LED
- No power at all
- Visible physical damage around BMC area

## Root Cause
Shipping damage affecting power delivery and BMC circuitry

## Resolution
Full RMA with photographic evidence

## Validation
- Replacement board showed normal IPMI LED and full remote access

## Lessons Learned
- Absence of IPMI LED on these boards is a reliable indicator of serious power/BMC damage

**Evidence:** IPMI LED photos and power-on attempts

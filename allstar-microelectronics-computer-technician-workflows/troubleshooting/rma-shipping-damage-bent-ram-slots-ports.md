# RMA: Shipping Damage – Bent RAM Slots & Physical Ports (H12DSi-N6)

**Status:** Resolved (RMA Issued)  
**Date:** March 2025

## Problem Summary
Board arrived with multiple bent RAM slots and damaged rear ports; would not POST.

## Findings
- Multiple P1/P2 DIMM slots bent/deformed
- Bent VGA/DisplayPort and loose chassis fan brackets
- Visible scuffing and chassis intrusion damage

## Root Cause
Shipping damage

## Resolution
- Full photographic documentation
- RMA submitted and board replaced

## Validation
- Replacement board passed full Memtest and burn-in

## Lessons Learned
- Document RAM slot and port damage separately — these are common hidden issues in shipping claims

**Evidence:** Close-up photos of bent DIMM slots and ports (in evidence/ folder)

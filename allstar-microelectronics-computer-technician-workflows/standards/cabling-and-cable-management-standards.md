# Standard: Cabling & Cable Management

- Small/low-profile cables (HD Audio, USB, front-panel) installed first.
- Main power cables (24-pin, CPU 8-pin, PCIe) installed next.
- Front-panel power/LED cables installed last.
- All cables routed flat through chassis channels and secured with Velcro.
- Serviceability and airflow are the top priorities.
- No twisted or “rat’s nest” bundles.

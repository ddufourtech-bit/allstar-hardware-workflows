# Standard: Fan Headers, CHA_FAN1, and Cooling

- CHA_FAN1 **must** be populated on every system.
- Water pumps connect to AIO_PUMP or CPU_FAN header.
- RGB lighting uses dedicated RGB headers only.
- Fan curves must be verified in BIOS/IPMI after build.
- Dust and airflow must be checked before any temperature-related troubleshooting.

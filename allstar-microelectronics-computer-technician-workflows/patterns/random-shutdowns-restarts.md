# Pattern: Random Shutdowns or Restarts

**Status:** Common Failure Mode  
**Date:** May 2025

## Pattern
- Usually caused by overheating.
- Can also be software errors or crashes.

## Diagnostic Workflow & Fixes
1. Check heatsink seating and thermal paste condition.
2. Verify all fans are spinning and not obstructed.
3. If cooling checks out → motherboard may be failing.

## Lessons Learned
- Overheating is the #1 cause — always verify cooling hardware before assuming a board failure.

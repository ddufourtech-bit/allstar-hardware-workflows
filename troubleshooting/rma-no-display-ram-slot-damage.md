# RMA: No Display – RAM Slot Physical Damage

**Status:** Resolved (RMA Issued)  
**Date:** March 2025

## Problem Summary
System powered on but no display output; RAM slots showed physical damage.

## Findings
- Visibly bent/deformed RAM slots on multiple channels
- RAM tested good on known-good board
- No POST codes or beeps

## Root Cause
Shipping-related physical damage to DIMM slots

## Resolution
Board submitted for RMA

## Validation
- Replacement board passed full RAM population and Memtest

## Lessons Learned
- Bent RAM slots can cause “no display” even when the board otherwise powers on

**Evidence:** Photos of damaged DIMM slots
